First Steps Checklist

If death occurred at Home:

Call 911 immediately if it was unexpected. If the person was under Hospice care, call the Hospice nurse.

If death occurred at a Hospital or Facility:

The staff will guide you. Let them know you have chosen Compassionate Care.

When to call us:

We are available 24/7 to bring your loved one into our care once authorities have given clearance.

When we meet, please try to bring:

  • Clothing for the deceased (including undergarments)
  • Social Security Number
  • Veteran's Discharge Papers (DD-214) if applicable
  • Basic Vital Statistics (Date of Birth, Parents' Names)
